The majority of sales in Birmingham during the last year were terraced properties, selling for an average price of £171,733. Semi-detached properties sold for an average of £213,703, with flats fetching £166,715.

Birmingham, with an overall average price of £202,619, was similar in terms of sold prices to nearby Birmingham City Centre (£206,256), but was cheaper than Edgbaston (£314,487) and Selly Park (£296,967).

Overall sold prices in Birmingham over the last year were similar to the previous year and 17% up on the 2016 level of £173,767.
